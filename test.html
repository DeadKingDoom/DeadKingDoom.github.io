Quick Tips & FAQs

Quick Tip: Monitor Growth Time

Ensure you enter the correct growth time for your crop. Accurate growth times lead to more precise yield and profit calculations.

Quick Tip: Use the Base Values Table

Refer to the crop base values table below for default sell prices and growth times. This helps verify your inputs and understand the calculations.

FAQ: How does the history work?

The list below tracks your last five calculations. These are stored locally in your browser and update after each calculation. Clear your browser data to reset the history.

FAQ: What is a yield multiplier?

The yield multiplier accounts for bonuses (like pets or events) that increase output. For example, a multiplier of 1.5 means a 50% increase in yield. Adjust this slider to match your current bonuses.
